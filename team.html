We are a French team that is passionate about technology, the web and what it can brings to society. We have all worked in the web industry for many years in agencies, startups or as freelancers. We are using our experience to build great products that we hope have a positive impact. We believe in more collaborative ways to create a company and we have chosen the cooperative model to form Digicoop. A company is made of people and we think giving every one of them a stake in it allows them to have a meaningful impact on its future.
